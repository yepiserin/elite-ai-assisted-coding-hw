Create Story Outline - MICE Structure

MICE Narrative Structure:

  • Milieu: Setting-focused. Begins when character enters new place, ends when they leave.
  • Inquiry: Question-focused. Begins with a question, ends with the answer.
  • Character: Character arc-focused. Begins with dissatisfaction, ends with change/acceptance.
  • Event: Plot-focused. Begins when world balance disrupted, ends when restored.
